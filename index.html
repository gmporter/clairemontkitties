Kittens for Adoption in Clairemont!

A feral momma cat in our neighborhood gave birth to a litter of kittens. There were four, but now there are three. We have been feeding them in the hopes of getting them medical care. Because we already have two cats, we cannot adopt them. We will be trapping them soon and covering all medical care, including shots, vaccines, and spaying/neutering.

They currently live in an area where there are numerous coyotes, hawks, and raccoons, and so they are not safe and secure for the long term. The rescue organization we are working with cannot take on any new cats because they are full, as is the Humane Society, and so we need your help. These kittens are approximately five months old, which is a great age for adoption!

If you or someone you know can take one or more of these kittens into your home, please get in touch at either +1 510 219 4164 or clairemontkitties@tikimail.org.
